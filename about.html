Our story

About Hilda May Events

We are an event planning and production studio. We lead concept, design, logistics, and on-site execution for weddings, corporate programs, and private celebrations. Catering is delivered by our culinary partners, with menus tailored to the brief.

Our name honors our founder’s great-grandmother, Hilda May—whose standards of generosity, elegance, and resolve shape how we work.

Our approach

Exceptional events are built through clarity, intention, and calm leadership. Our role is to hold the full picture — creative vision, timing, logistics, vendor alignment, and guest experience — so you can be fully present.

We design events that feel thoughtful rather than overproduced. Every decision supports the experience: spatial flow, pacing, transitions, and the details your guests will remember.

We collaborate closely with venues, chefs, and production partners to deliver a seamless and polished result — executed with care, precision, and warmth.
