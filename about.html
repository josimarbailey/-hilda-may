Our Story

Hilda May Events was founded to bring together culinary artistry and event design with precision logistics. Inspired by over 15 years in luxury hospitality, we craft experiences that balance timeless aesthetics with modern execution.

Our founder named the company after her great‑grandmother, Hilda May, whose values of generosity, elegance, and resilience continue to guide our work today.

Philosophy

Every event is a story. We approach each with a blank canvas, then collaborate with our clients to shape menus, atmospheres, and flow that reflect their priorities. Our team blends interior design, hospitality, and fine arts backgrounds to ensure that both the culinary and visual details harmonize seamlessly.
