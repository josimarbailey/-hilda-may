Food with purpose

Our culinary team is led by Chef Josie Singh, blending classical technique with vibrant global flavours. We plan every menu alongside your program flow—making sure service, pacing, and guest experience are effortless.

Inclusive by design: Every menu is mapped with clear dietary icons, cross-contamination controls, and thoughtful alternatives for vegan, halal, kosher-style, gluten-free, nut-free, and allergy-sensitive guests.

Responsible sourcing: We work with Ontario farms, sustainable fisheries, and ethical purveyors. Seasonal availability guides our menus, and we prioritise waste reduction through smart forecasting and compostable serviceware.

Polished service: Uniformed service captains, sommeliers, and culinary leads execute to a detailed run-of-show prepared in tandem with Hilda May’s production team.

Dietary, safety & sustainability

Every detail is documented in the same production playbooks used across Hilda May Events.

  • Dietary mapping: Ingredient matrices, clear labeling, and dedicated prep zones for allergen-sensitive items.
  • Food safety: HACCP-aligned protocols, refrigerated transport, and traceability from supplier to service.
  • Sustainability: Composting partnerships, post-event donations, and measurement of diversion metrics.
